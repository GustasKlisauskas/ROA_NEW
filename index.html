INTEGRATION

Is a unique fighting game on Avalanche(AVAX) blockchain which involves skill and planning to play. It is a game resembling Tekken, Street Fighter, and Mortal Combat. The main difference, most character and guild data is saved on the blockchain, and you can earn crypto playing it. It also has some RPG elements to it. When fighting other players and participating in tournaments, your level and rank will increase.

And so will your statistical attributes, such as:

Strength - Character will improve to deal more damage. Agility - You will be faster when moving side to side or backward and forwards. Intelligence - will fill the unique gauge to the max faster and use your special character abilities more quickly. Nobility - More of it will allow the timer to use a character-specific weapon longer.

REVIVAL OD AVALANCHE FEATURES

It will help you earn more RVVL token then you would on your own, in most circumstances. A Weekly Draw X will be distributed to the best performing guild of the week. Guild master will decide to leave it in a treasury or distribute it evenly to all team members. 60% of the guild members will have to vote on a guild master decision. Every single guild will mint their own smart contract with a treasury and a DAO. Guild master will be able to mint guild NFTs and distribute it to the players which in turn automatically will have an access to the guild.

Participating in tournaments, you can win a lot more RVVL tokens as an individual player, even if you are a guild member. Being a guild master allows you to create a tournament with a bigger betting size per person, and only those in similar or stronger guilds will respond. Way harder, but potential rewards are ludicrous. It will be much more common to see tournaments of 1000 RVVL per player than those guild-based ones, but it will be EPIC to participate and watch guild tournaments! Betting will be possible in ROA metaverse collaborations too!
Is a guild’s chest, where Revival (RVVL) tokens are collected by a guild. It can be used to buy NFT gadgets if there are enough REVIVAL tokens in a treasury and be borrowed by guild members for some time. Rank increase within the guild will allow more time to borrow items. Guild members can boost their more robust, more skillful comraderies to win easier in the battles. It will also bring home more RVVL tokens for their team and win more for themselves as betting size increases. For example, a homeless cyborg(no guild) up to level 10 can bet 1000 RVVL tokens against the other cyborg. That is good enough for some, but more giant whales might want to bet significantly more, so you would either buy a guild or join one. Guilds will collect RESPECT X points and unlock better features for their guilds. There is also a possibility to win NFTs and other valuables by collecting RESPECT X.
Searching for armor, weapon, shield, and boots NFTs within the game dashboard or Kalao marketplace will further boost your attributes. Watch your equipment combinations when upgrading your characters that fit your fighting style.
A great way to earn a passive income if you are a seasoned whale and don’t want to or don’t have time to play and gain that precious RVVL yourself. Renting doesn’t mean you will have to transfer your NFTs to another wallet. It basically means you won’t be able to play, while the other person will be enjoying their experience fighting others for a rent period. You will reap the rewards of gaining experience, collecting rent fees, and 10% of all winnings. Betting losses will be covered only by a person renting your NFT character. Still, Win/Loss ratio will be stored on blockchain regardless of who is playing. Anyone can delete Win/Loss ratio at any time, but it will cost gas fees.
