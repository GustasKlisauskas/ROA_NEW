INTEGRATION

Is a unique fighting game on Avalanche(AVAX) blockchain which involves skill and planning to play. It is a game resembling Tekken, Street Fighter, and Mortal Kombat. The main difference, most character and guild data is saved on the blockchain, and you can earn crypto playing it. It also has some RPG elements to it. When fighting other players and participating in tournaments, your level and rank will increase.

REVIVAL OD AVALANCHE FEATURES

It will help you earn more $RVVL token than you would on your own, in most circumstances. A Weekly Draw X will be distributed to the best performing guild of the week. Guild master will decide to leave it in a treasury or distribute it evenly to all team members. 60% of the guild members will have to vote on a guild master’s decision. Every single guild will mint their own smart contract with a treasury and a DAO. Guild master will be able to mint guild member NFTs and distribute it to the players wallets which in turn automatically will have an access to the guild.

GAME REWARDS

Various events and explorations in the game will provide rewards to the players in the form of REVIVAL Token.

WEEKLY DRAW X S-CONTRACT

0.7% is distributed to the best-performing guild every week. Guild with the most RESPECT X points in a week will receive the bonus of 0.7% Swap fees in their treasury.

WEEKLY DRAW S-CONTRACT

0.3% is distributed to the best performing individual player of the week. An individual with the most RESPECT points earns a bonus of 0.3% Swap fees in their treasury.

Buy back and burn

10% of all NFT sales will be sent to the BBB pool, used for significant orders to be repurchased from sellers, and sent to the dead wallet, maintaining a healthy $RVVL chart.

FUTURE of Revival Token

Revival token is programmed so that we as developers can change the distribution of dex fees however we see fit, up to 7% max. So once we start building more games as Newton Mint labs, the Revival token will become a governance token through changing dex fees to support future ecosystem. We will deploy a new token called Revived, which will have the same distribution as the Revival token at the game's launch. Revived token sole purpose will be to serve the Revival of Avalanche's in-game economy. In contrast, the Revival token will become a governance token for all of the projects we will be bringing to the world. Investing in Revival is investing in all our NFT and game projects.

Three months or more of locked Revival staking will automatically reward staker with a Revived token.

STAKING Revival token

Two staking modes will be available upon launching the game. Solo staking and liquidity staking. Both will reward stakers with more Revival tokens and give stakers in-game benefits. (The in-game benefits will differ depending on solo or liquidity staking) We will deploy Revived token in the future to support the in-game economy instead of a Revival token. An investor will continue staking a Revival token for in-game rewards instead of a newly deployed Revived token. There will be choices, what an investor wants to receive as a reward: Revival (governance) token, or Revived (in-game economy supporting) token.
